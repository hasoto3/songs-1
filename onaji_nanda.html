Someday These Tears Will Dry

同じなんだ

大森 カエ

今、生きている私から。
過去の、死をいつも考えていた私へ。

今の苦しみは、
生きてさえいれば、いつか宝物に変わる。

From me, who is living now —
to the me who always thought of dying.

The pain you carry now will, if only you keep living, someday become a treasure.

There are nights when you wake alone in the dark, wondering why you are even alive. Those nights, I think, do not belong to you alone. We are all the same.
